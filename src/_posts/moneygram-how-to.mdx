The [Stellar MoneyGram Access](https://stellar.org/moneygram) is available in 175+ countries and more than 300k locations! Here is the [list of supported countries](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1batl_ykVzF9czFpYoW3zYDSLaHu4S3KnaFUoYaS-XdM/edit#gid=0).

### How do you use MoneyGram Access&trade; with the Decaf Wallet?

#### How to withdraw your USDC with cash

1. Open your Decaf Wallet app and click "Withdraw" on your Cash Account.
2. Select "Withdraw cash at MoneyGram" as your withdrawal method.
   Enter your country location and the amount of USDC you wish to withdraw.
3. Confirm the transaction details and submit to send the USDC to MoneyGram.
4. You will be assigned a reference number from Decaf Wallet.
5. Go to the designated MoneyGram location and provide your ID and reference number to pick up your cash.

Did you know you can buy USDC with cash at MoneyGram locations in the US using the [Decaf Wallet](https://www.decaf.so/wallet)? There are 0 fees, so $1 cash = 1 USDC.

After receiving USDC in your Decaf Wallet you can use Solana Pay! This article will show you how.

Here’s how to buy USDC with cash at MoneyGram:

1. Select “Buy USDC with MoneyGram”.
2. Enter the cash deposit amount and select the location along with your personal details and ID info.
3. Once at MoneyGram, show your ID and give your cash, and in just a few minutes, you will see the USDC in your green card (Cash Account)!
